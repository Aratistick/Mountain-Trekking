2. Rever Rafting

6. Paragliding

- Paragliding in India has been one of the most loved adventure activities as it unleashes the adventurer in you and lets you relish upon numerous views of the surrounding nature ranging from snow capped hills to vast seasides. The paragliding sessions at places like Nandi Hills and Kamshet will give you a chance to catch the glimpses of the lofty hills of the Western Ghats and the beautiful terrains and topographies.

- Other than the serenity, the thrill, the rush of adrenaline and the racing heart which one feels here has no comparison. With the combination of high altitude and artificial wings, there is a lot to take in as you soar above. Paragliding is all about elevation and the views which one catches from above, therefore paragliding places in India like Baliman Dara let you enjoy the picturesque views of Sikkim, and Kerala promises you the enchanting viewpoints of shoreline, foliage, valleys and hills.
